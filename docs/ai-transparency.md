# AI Transparency

> 🤖 **AI Transparency Notice:** This document was written with AI assistance (Claude by Anthropic). The decisions, requirements and validations are human-driven; the drafting and structuring were AI-assisted. See [AI Transparency](./ai-transparency.md) for details.

This document explains how Artificial Intelligence was used throughout the development of this project, and what remained entirely human-driven.

Transparency about AI usage is important to us. Just as the Linux kernel community recently sparked debate around AI-generated contributions, we believe any open source project should be clear and honest about the role AI plays in its creation. This is not a disclaimer — it is a feature.

---

## Philosophy

AI is used here as a tool, not a replacement for human judgement. Every decision — what to build, what rules to apply, what to include or exclude — is made by a human. AI assists in turning those decisions into well-structured text and working code, faster and more consistently than doing it alone.

Think of it like a compiler: you write the logic, the tool handles the boilerplate.

---

## What was AI-assisted

*The human was in control. AI helped with drafting, structuring and wording.*

**Documentation** is the main area where AI assistance was used. Requirements, rules and decisions were defined by the human through a structured conversation with Claude. Claude then helped turn those into well-structured, consistently worded documents.

Whenever a document was written with AI assistance, it carries the following notice at the top:

> 🤖 **AI Transparency Notice:** This document was written with AI assistance (Claude by Anthropic). The decisions, requirements and validations are human-driven; the drafting and structuring were AI-assisted. See [AI Transparency](./ai-transparency.md) for details.

---

## What was AI-generated

*AI was in control. The human reviewed, validated and approved.*

| Artifact | Tool | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Source code | Claude Code (Anthropic) | The entire codebase was generated by Claude Code based on the functional specifications and acceptance tests. The human reviewed the output, ran the tests, and approved the final result. |
| GitLab CI/CD configuration | Claude Code (Anthropic) | Pipeline configuration generated by Claude Code and validated by the human. |

---

## What is 100% human

- The idea and motivation behind the project
- All functional decisions (rules, scope, edge cases)
- Validation of every AI-generated artifact
- Final approval before any code is merged or published
